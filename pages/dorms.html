UC Davis Dorm Information

Housing will be at Tercero Residence Halls in Laben Hall at
    458 Dairy Rd, Davis, CA 95616

Dining will be offered at Segundo Dining Commons at
    663 Sprocket Bikeway, Davis, CA 95616

Three meals are provided for you as part of housing. Even though there is a dining commons in the Tercero dorm area, that will not be open for you to use.

The Segundo Dining Commons will be your designated dining commons area. This is a 12 minute walk from Laben Hall.

Check-In Information

Check-In time is 4pm on the day of arrival at the Tercero Services Center on the first floor. The Tercero Service Desk is staffed until 11:00 pm.

Late arrivals need to call (530)752-2900 once you arrive in Davis so the on-call staff can arrange a time to meet you outside the Tercero Services Center (next to Tercero Market).
