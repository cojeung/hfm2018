Getting to Davis

Davis Airporter

Offers pick up from
  • San Francisco International Airport (SFO)
    • ($85 for first person; then $35 for each additional person)
  • Sacramento International Airport
    • ($23 for first person; then $12 for each additional person)
Set destination to UC Davis at
    1 Shields Ave, Davis, CA 95616
or any destination in Davis.

Must book in advance.

BART + Amtrak (Price varies)

From Oakland International Airport (OAK) or San Francisco International Airport (SFO) to Davis

  • First take BART to Richmond station
    • If landing at OAK, nearest BART station is located across from Terminal 1 baggage claim area.
    • If landing at SFO, nearest BART station is located at the International Terminal. BART station is also accessible by AirTrain from all other terminals.
  • From Richmond station, take Amtrak from Richmond to Davis.

From Davis Amtrak Station to UC Davis Campus

Note: Most hotels are at walking distance from Davis Amtrak station.
  • Unitrans (local campus bus system)
    • Please take A-line (see schedule here)
    • Or Z-line (see schedule here)
  • Ride share such as Uber or Lyft (see below)

Ride Sharing (Price varies)

Uber or Lyft (App-Based Ride Sharing)

  • Must download beforehand onto mobile or tablet
  • Create an account to use
  • Supports only debit/credit card or PayPal
Similar to international apps―such as BlaBlaCar, Careem, Curb, Didi Chuxing, Flywheel, Grab, Haxi, MyTaxi, and more.
